User story based on roles:
	1. As a guest, i want to browse near by user.
	2. As a guest, i want to sign up for account.
	3  As a member, i want to sign in my account.
	4. As a member, i want browse near by user.
	5. As a member, i want to display my location on map.
	6. As a member, i want to view profile of a user.
	7. As a member, i want to send interest to another user.
	8. As a member, i want to send message to another user.
	9. As a member, i want to logout.
